Hi, I'm Aldrin—a Computer Science student at Holy Angel University and a Full-Stack Developer currently interning at Geo Pro Global Solutions.

My approach to tech is a bit like a puzzle. To build great software, I believe you need to understand how the pieces connect—from the high-level UI down to the wires. That's why I've spent the last few years rounding out my coding with a deep dive into networking, from CCNA Introduction to Networks all the way to Switching, Routing, and Wireless Essentials.

I'm a big believer in staying "student-mode forever." I started with PCAP Programming Essentials back in 2022, and I'm capping off March 2026 by finalizing my Python Certification from freeCodeCamp.

Beyond code, I'm a filmmaker (short film competition winner with Final Cut Pro), indie game builder, and—someday—the founder of a forest-themed café in Angeles City. I build things that are technically sharp and deeply human-centered.
